About Us

We've been making healthy food last for 10 years

At Bucky Cafe, we make every last bite from scratch, using fresh ingredients from close to home and serving healthy food that takes you to a happy place. That’s our promise to you, whether you’re vegetarian, vegan, paleo, or just hungry. And just because we can do sugar-free, dairy-free and gluten-free doesn’t mean we do flavor-free… because where’s the happy in that? We’re on a simple, soul-satisfying mission to radiate positively delicious vibes. So whether you’re in the mood to relax and dine in, or just grabbing a quick healthy bite to go, join us and nourish your mind, body and soul.

What Our Customers Say

Ross Lee

The Bucky Café is a gem for health enthusiasts. I tried their avocado toast with a side of fruit salad, and it was a delicious and nutritious breakfast. The café's atmosphere is bright and inviting, perfect for starting the day on a healthy note. Prices are reasonable, and the service is quick. I'll be back for sure.

Amelia Watson

I'm not vegan, but I thoroughly enjoyed my meal at Bucky Café. Their vegan burger was surprisingly flavorful and satisfying. The restaurant has a trendy vibe, and the staff is friendly. It's a great place to explore plant-based dining options, even for non-vegans like me.

Ben Roy

If you're in the mood for a fresh and filling salad, Bucky Café is the place to go. I ordered the Mediterranean salad, and it was packed with colorful veggies and a tasty dressing. The portions are generous, making it a great lunch spot. The only downside is limited indoor seating, but the food makes up for it.
